--- layout: post title: Last day of school, in 2008 ---

On the last day of the school year it is common practice in the Netherlands that the building is transformed by the graduate students. There is a theme that has been kept secret for months, and an afternoon show to amuse the fellow students and teachers, usually with some pranks on the latter.

In 2008 our class graduated, and we decided to hold a hippie festival. Unfortunately, it wasn’t as popular as Woodstock about forty years earlier, but still it was totally awesome! A few weeks ago I found all the video material of that day and decided to make a compilation. The first video is about the morning, giving an impression of the great atmosphere in the school. The second video is the show, which is entirely in Dutch.
